---
title: "Confidence"
---

How well-anchored your knowledge is, at a glance. The top bar shows the overall distribution of confidence levels across every page that has one. Below it, the same breakdown per subject and per page type. Pages with no `confidence:` field at all show as a small gray strip at the right of each bar — a calibration data-quality signal in its own right.

The lint workflow flags pages missing this field; this view shows you where the calibration is uneven, where you're confidently anchored, and where things are still speculative.
