title: "Tags"
---

Tags that travel together. Each node is one tag; the size shows how many pages use it. Edges connect tags that appear on the same page, with thicker edges for stronger co-occurrence. Gold nodes are tags that are also subjects in the controlled vocabulary; gray-green nodes are tag-only — the ones to watch as candidates for promotion if they cluster strongly with the gold core. Hover a tag for its details; click to see every page filed under it.
