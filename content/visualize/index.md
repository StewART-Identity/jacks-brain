title: "Visualize"
---

Five views of your collection. Each surfaces something the static page rendering can't:

- **[[visualize/confidence|Confidence]]** — how well-anchored your knowledge is, at a glance.
- **[[visualize/graph|Graph]]** — the wiki-link network. Pages connect to the pages they reference.
- **[[visualize/subjects|Subjects]]** — the shape of your ontology, sized by page count.
- **[[visualize/tags|Tags]]** — tags that travel together, with promotion candidates.
- **[[visualize/timeline|Timeline]]** — when you cataloged what, across types.
