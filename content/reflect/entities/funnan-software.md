FunnanSoftware is a YouTube channel producing short-form programming content focused on C++ and software engineering best practices. Videos are framed as advice for working developers, frequently using interview scenarios to illustrate what distinguishes junior from senior knowledge — a "this is what separates them" structure that turns abstract concepts into concrete hiring signals.
