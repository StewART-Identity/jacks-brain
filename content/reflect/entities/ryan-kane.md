Ryan Kane is a supervisor within the IAM team at UNT System, appearing in Jack Stewart's Microsoft Teams contact list and functioning as a direct manager or department lead in the identity management organization.

## Role and responsibilities

Based on the April 2026 conversation documented in [[reflect/sources/2026-05-24-pasted-image-1779669099737]], Kane is involved in:

- **Entra project oversight** — Jack describes himself as the "working" architect for the Entra project "and IAM team," suggesting Kane holds organizational authority over both
- **Meeting materials** — Kane removed "IAM Architect" from meeting materials upon issuing his guidance, indicating he has editorial authority over official IAM team communications
- **Title and role governance** — Kane has visibility into what titles EIS (UNT System's employee information system) carries, and communicates the boundary between informal working titles and formally conferred ones

## Title guidance, April 2026

In an April 13, 2026 Teams message, Kane instructed Jack Stewart to stop using "IAM Architect" on formal documents, signatures, and materials until the title is formalized in EIS. Kane indicated there was "no timeline at the moment" for that formalization. He described the title as "something on the radar but not clearly defined" — acknowledging it as a goal without committing to a deadline.

This conversation is the authoritative record behind the wiki's convention that Jack's title is **IAM Engineer**. See [[reflect/concepts/working-title]] for the broader concept this illustrates.
