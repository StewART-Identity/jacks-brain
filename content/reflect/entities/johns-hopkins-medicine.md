Johns Hopkins Medicine is the integrated medical enterprise of The Johns Hopkins University and The Johns Hopkins Hospital, headquartered in Baltimore, Maryland. It encompasses medical education, research programs, and a health system spanning multiple hospitals and clinical facilities.

## Role in this wiki

Johns Hopkins Medicine is the publisher of the [[reflect/sources/2026-05-18-brain-anatomy-and-how-the-brain-works-johns-hopkins-medicine|brain anatomy reference]] used as the primary source for [[reflect/concepts/brain-anatomy]] and [[reflect/concepts/central-nervous-system]]. Their health content is written for a general medical audience and reviewed by clinical faculty.

## Notable areas

Hopkins is internationally recognized in neurology, neurosurgery, oncology, and cardiology, and has historically contributed foundational research across most medical disciplines. The institution's public health content (including the brain anatomy reference in this wiki) is widely cited in educational and clinical contexts.
