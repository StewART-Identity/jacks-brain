## Conversation — April 13

Ryan Kane opens by asking Jack not to use "IAM Architect" on formal documents or signatures until the title is formalized in EIS. When Jack asks when that formalization might occur, Kane responds there is "no timeline at the moment." Jack notes that when he joined, he was told the title was "actively being pursued." Kane clarifies that something can be actively pursued without a definitive deadline — framing it as "something on the radar but not clearly defined" — and adds that he has already removed "IAM Architect" from the relevant meeting materials.

Jack explains he had been using the title informally because people were asking questions about his role, and that he had been telling them it was a work in progress consistent with whatever EIS showed.

## Conversation — April 23

Jack adds that he had assumed it was common knowledge that he was the "working" architect for the Entra project and the IAM team, but states he understands now. Kane responds that he hopes the suggestion didn't disrupt anything, and offers to simply monitor the relevant meetings going forward.
