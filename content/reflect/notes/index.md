title: "Notes"
---

Quick captures. Things worth jotting down before they slip — half-formed thoughts, observations, follow-ups, fragments. Once saved, each note becomes a Quartz page like any other: `[[wikilinks]]` and `#tags` are picked up by the build, backlinks accumulate, and the graph view shows the connections.

The form below writes directly to the repo. The page rebuilds and the graph refreshes on the next Cloudflare Pages deploy (~30 seconds).
