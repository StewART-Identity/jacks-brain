title: "Reflect"
---

What cataloging produces. Four page types, each capturing a different shape of knowledge.

- **[[reflect/concepts|Concepts]]** — ideas, theories, frameworks, principles. The abstractions that recur across sources.
- **[[reflect/entities|Entities]]** — people, organizations, tools, systems. The actors and things sources reference.
- **[[reflect/sources|Sources]]** — a summary page for every cataloged document. The faithful record of what the source says, with its role and re-view history.
- **[[reflect/synthesis|Synthesis]]** — cross-cutting analysis. Where sources connect, contrast, or accumulate into something new.
