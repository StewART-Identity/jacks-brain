title: "Collect"
---

The cataloging pipeline. Three pages, one per stage — from choosing what to add through to the permanent audit log.

- **[[collect/acquisition|Acquisition]]** — watch sources move through cataloging. Pending, in progress, cataloged, or failed.
- **[[collect/retention|Retention]]** — the chronological audit log of every cataloged source. Inline-editable titles.
- **[[collect/selection|Selection]]** — add a chosen source to the collection. Drop a file or URL; the cataloger picks it up.
