Manuel "Jack" Stewart II is an IAM Engineer based in Louisville, Kentucky. He has 29 years of experience in higher education IT, progressing from LAN and directory services work at the [[collection/entities/university-of-louisville]] (1994–2012) through a decade of IAM application development at the [[collection/entities/university-of-michigan]] (2012–2023), and into a senior engineering and architecture role at the [[collection/entities/university-of-north-texas]] (2024–present).

## Areas of Expertise

- **Identity provisioning**: Designing and implementing synchronization pipelines between authoritative sources and downstream systems using [[collection/entities/netiq-identity-manager]] and Azure AD Connect.
- **Directory services**: Deep experience with [[collection/concepts/active-directory]], OpenLDAP, and NetIQ eDirectory — including administering a ~1-million-object AD domain with a distributed admin model.
- **Cloud identity**: Led multiple [[collection/concepts/entra-id]] implementations; performed tenant-to-tenant migrations; authored cross-team run-books.
- **Federated authentication**: Processed SSO requests using [[collection/concepts/saml]] via Shibboleth (Internet 2 implementation).
- **Privileged access management**: Subject matter expertise in CyberArk PAM and Application Identity Manager.
- **Infrastructure automation**: Ansible for provisioning BIG-IP, Linux, and Windows infrastructure.
