Tim Howes was a software engineer at [[collection/entities/netscape-communications|Netscape Communications Corp.]] and a central contributor to the [[collection/concepts/ldap|LDAP]] standards effort. He is listed as co-author on RFC 2251 (LDAPv3 core protocol) and RFC 2252 (attribute syntax definitions), sole author of RFC 2254 ([[collection/sources/2026-05-02-rfc2254-txt|The String Representation of LDAP Search Filters]]), and co-author of RFC 2696 ([[collection/sources/2026-05-02-rfc2696-txt|LDAP Control Extension for Simple Paged Results]], September 1999) alongside [[collection/entities/microsoft|Microsoft Corp.]] engineers.

At the time of the December 1997 RFC publications, Howes was based at Netscape's Mountain View, CA office (501 E. Middlefield Road). His email address on record was `howes@netscape.com`.
