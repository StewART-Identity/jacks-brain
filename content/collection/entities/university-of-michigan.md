The University of Michigan (U-M) in Ann Arbor is one of the nation's leading public research universities. [[collection/entities/jack-stewart]] worked in U-M's Identity and Access Management division from March 2012 to October 2023 — an eleven-year tenure spanning three roles and both operational support and complex project delivery.

## Roles Held by Jack

| Period | Role | Team |
|--------|------|------|
| 2022–2023 | Application Developer Lead | IAM Operations |
| 2013–2022 | Application Developer Lead | IAM Line of Business |
| 2012–2013 | Client Server Programmer Senior | IAM Operations |

## Notable IAM Work at U-M

- **Active Directory domain administration** (2022–2023): Led administration of a ~1-million-object domain; promoted domain controllers via Ansible; deployed controllers to the Azure cloud and Dearborn campus; upgraded Certificate Services using an offline root CA / online issuing CA structure.
- **TeamDynamix integration** (2020): Built a custom [[collection/entities/netiq-identity-manager]] scripting driver and Python scripts to provision users and groups from eDirectory into TeamDynamix.
- **Azure AD implementations** (2017–2019, 2022): Led two consecutive [[collection/concepts/entra-id]] deployments; performed swing upgrade of Azure AD Connect v2; authored complex cross-team run-books for a tenant-to-tenant migration.

The Michigan environment spanned [[collection/concepts/active-directory]], [[collection/concepts/entra-id]], CyberArk, [[collection/entities/netiq-identity-manager]], Passwordstate, Shibboleth, and Splunk-based monitoring.
