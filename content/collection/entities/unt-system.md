The University of North Texas System (UNT System) is a multi-campus higher-education organization. The wiki's primary domain is the UNT System's identity and access management (IAM) infrastructure — including its [[collection/entities/microsoft-entra-id]] tenant, [[collection/concepts/authentication-methods|authentication method]] policies, and related tooling.

## IAM Environment

- **Identity platform:** [[collection/entities/microsoft-entra-id]]
- **Admin tooling:** [[collection/entities/dstools]]
- **MFA provider:** Duo (via native Microsoft authentication, post-migration from legacy MyUNT)
- **Staff MFA enrollment portal:** https://mfa.untsystem.edu
