The University of North Texas (UNT) is a public research university in Denton, Texas, and part of the University of North Texas System. In the context of this wiki, UNT is the current employer of [[collection/entities/jack-stewart]], who joined in February 2024 as IAM Engineer within the Identity and Access Management division.

Jack's role at UNT carries an explicit architecture dimension — his external title includes "Senior Engineer and Architect" — reflecting the broader industry shift as [[collection/concepts/entra-id]] absorbs more of what on-premise [[collection/concepts/active-directory]] and [[collection/entities/netiq-identity-manager]] once handled. The IAM practitioner role at this level increasingly spans platform design and governance, not just connector development.
