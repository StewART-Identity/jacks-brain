Self-directed study material organized by topic. Each topic carries its own landing page and a tree of concept pages; quiz questions live on the concept pages themselves and aggregate into the wiki-wide [[quiz/take|Quiz]].

- **[[upskill/add|Add]]** — register a new topic. Creates a sidebar entry and a landing page under `/upskill/<slug>/`.
- **[[upskill/update|Update]]** — edit, hide, or remove existing topics.
- **[[upskill/topics|Topics]]** — every topic in the wiki, as a card grid.
