title: "Journal"
---

Internal observations. Reflections on what you've gathered, the voice you bring to it, your own thinking taking shape over time. The companion to [[notes|Notes]], which holds the external side.

- **[[journal/browse|Browse]]** — every journal entry you've written so far. Each row expands inline for editing without leaving the page.
- **[[journal/entries/|entries]]** — the entries themselves. New captures land here automatically.
- **[[journal/write|Write]]** — a fresh entry. Title, body, optional tags.
