---
title: "Notes"
---

Observations of the world. Things you've read, references you want to keep, fragments and glossaries about what's outside you. The companion to [[journal|Journal]], which holds the internal side.

- **[[notes/browse|Browse]]** — every note you've written so far. Each row expands inline for editing without leaving the page.
- **[[notes/entries/|entries]]** — the notes themselves. New captures land here automatically.
- **[[notes/write|Write]]** — a fresh capture. Title, body, optional tags.
