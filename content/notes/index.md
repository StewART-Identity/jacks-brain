Your own captures, alongside everything you've gathered from elsewhere.

- **[[notes/browse|Browse]]** — every note you've written so far. Each row expands inline for editing without leaving the page.
- **[[notes/journal/|journal]]** — the notes themselves. New captures land here automatically.
- **[[notes/write|Write]]** — a fresh capture. Title, body, optional tags.
