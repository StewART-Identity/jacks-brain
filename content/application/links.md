---
title: "Links"
---

Manage the links published on your shared links page. Group links into sections, then add, edit, reorder, or remove them. Changes are saved to the wiki and published on the next deploy.

---

After saving, review your changes on the **[[application/preview|Preview]]** page — it shows every link with the public ones marked and the private ones flagged, so you can confirm what will reach the public page.
