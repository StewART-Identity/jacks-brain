# Internet2

Internet2 is a nonprofit consortium of U.S. research and education institutions that operates a high-speed network and provides shared technology services to higher education. It manages collaborative programs including the [[entities/incommon|InCommon]] identity federation and the [[entities/eduroam|eduroam]] roaming Wi-Fi service in the United States.

## Role in eduroam

Internet2 operates the U.S. national [[concepts/radius|RADIUS]] infrastructure for [[entities/eduroam|eduroam]], hosting workshops and training for institutional technical staff. Jack Stewart attended Internet2's eduroam Technical RADIUS Workshop in March 2026 as a registered InCommon Participant from the University of North Texas System.
