# Internet2

Internet2 is a non-profit research and education networking consortium serving U.S. universities and research institutions. It operates advanced networking infrastructure and manages several identity and access management programs widely used in higher education.

## Relevance to UNT

[[entities/unt|UNT]] is an Internet2 member institution, qualifying Jack Stewart to register as an [[entities/incommon|InCommon]] Participant at Internet2-hosted events. UNT staff have attended Internet2 training workshops, including the [[concepts/radius|RADIUS]]-focused [[entities/eduroam|eduroam]] workshop (March 4–5, 2026).

## Key Programs

- **[[entities/incommon|InCommon]]** — Identity federation for higher education, operated by Internet2
- **[[entities/eduroam|eduroam]]** — Wi-Fi roaming service for research and education networks
- **Training & Workshops** — Technical workshops on networking, identity, and security topics
