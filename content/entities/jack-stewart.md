# Jack Stewart

Architect/Engineer, Identity and Access Management at [[entities/unt-system]].

## Role

Jack Stewart is the primary author and technical lead for [[entities/unt-system]]'s IAM engineering work. He prepared the [[concepts/conditional-access-policy]] and [[concepts/external-authentication-method]] rollout strategy documented in the Entra Authentication Methods Rollout Plan.

## Known Work

- Authored the MFA Reauthentication Frequency & System-Preferred MFA rollout plan (March 2026), covering [[concepts/system-preferred-mfa]] disablement, [[concepts/mfa-sign-in-frequency]] configuration, and [[entities/citrix-horizon]] MFA enforcement via [[entities/microsoft-entra-id]].
- Led the post-migration remediation planning following the January 28, 2026 ADFS → Entra ID migration.
