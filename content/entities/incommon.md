# InCommon

InCommon is the U.S. higher education identity federation, operated by [[entities/internet2|Internet2]]. Member institutions agree to common identity assurance standards, enabling federated single sign-on (SSO) across participating universities, research organizations, and service providers.

## Relationship to eduroam

InCommon participation is the gateway for U.S. institutions to join [[entities/eduroam|eduroam]]. The University of North Texas System is an InCommon Participant, which is reflected in Jack Stewart's registration category for the eduroam Technical [[concepts/radius|RADIUS]] Workshop in March 2026.

## Relevance to UNT System Identity Infrastructure

As an InCommon member, UNT System operates a compliant identity provider (IdP), which integrates with [[entities/active-directory|Active Directory]], [[entities/entra-id|Entra ID]], and other identity systems to support federated access.
