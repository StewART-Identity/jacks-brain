# InCommon

InCommon is a federated identity management service operated by [[entities/internet2|Internet2]] for U.S. research and education institutions. Member institutions agree to common trust and attribute-sharing standards, enabling single sign-on (SSO) and service access across institutions without per-institution bilateral agreements.

## Relevance to UNT

[[entities/unt|UNT]] is an InCommon Participant. This status is used as a registration category for Internet2-hosted events and grants access to InCommon-federated services. Jack Stewart's registration for the [[entities/eduroam|eduroam]] Technical [[concepts/radius|RADIUS]] Workshop (March 4–5, 2026) listed him under the "InCommon Participant" category.

## Relationship to Other Entities

- Operated by [[entities/internet2|Internet2]]
- Enables federated access to [[entities/eduroam|eduroam]] and other shared services
- Complements [[entities/idtree|IDTREE]]/LDAP-based local identity management at UNT
