## Key Observations

- **Domain Admins** is a built-in [[concepts/ad-security-groups|AD Global Security group]] that grants full administrative privileges over the domain.
- The group is **not protected from accidental deletion**, which is a notable security posture gap — Microsoft best practice recommends enabling deletion protection on privileged groups.
- No owners are listed, meaning there is no designated steward responsible for membership governance.
- The group has existed since July 2007, predating most modern IAM tooling in the environment.
